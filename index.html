Pitch Wobblies!

Musical Ear Training

A colourful, playful ear training game for children and families. Listen closely, toss fruit, and train your ears by befriending a cast of colorful, singing Wobblies.

Support: pitchwobblies@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2026

Studio Io ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. We built Pitch Wobblies! as a safe, educational environment for children to learn and play.

1. Data Collection and Use

This app is designed for children and families. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

2. Local Save Data

All game progress, such as unlocked levels, audio settings, and collected Blobs, is saved locally directly on your device. We do not sync, transmit, or have access to this data on any external servers.

3. Advertising and Analytics

Pitch Wobblies does not contain any third-party advertising, and we do not use third-party analytics trackers to monitor player behavior.

4. External Links

Any links to outside resources in Pitch Wobblies! (such as our developer website or support email) are protected behind a Parental Gate (a mathematical lock) to ensure children cannot accidentally leave the app or access the internet without adult supervision.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

6. Contact Us

If you are a parent or guardian and have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
